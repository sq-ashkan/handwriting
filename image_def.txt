Standard Specifications for Dataset Processing and Unification:

Image Output:
- Dimensions: Exactly 27×27 pixels
- Color Scheme: white characters on black background (0 for black, 255 for white)
- File Format: PNG with grayscale channel

Image Processing:
- Gaussian: sigma=0.3 (به جای 0.5)
- CLAHE: clipLimit=3.0, tileSize=(3,3)
- Adaptive Threshold: blockSize=9, C=3
- نسبت اشغال: 65-75% (برای خوانایی بهتر)

Quality:
- Clarity: Sharp edges without broken pixels
- Continuity: No line breaks
- Uniformity: Even pixel distribution in line thickness
- Detail Preservation: Maintain key character features
